Flank Steak

From "Cow" to "Wow"

When it comes to a dinner centerpiece that combines health and satisfaction, steak is a tough entree to beat. I opted to include my Flank Steak preparation as this cut contains a particularly admirable beef taste. Check out the world of steak cuts here.

Preparation

1. Gather your Seaoning and Let Steak Sit
Remove steak from the fridge and let it sit out so it can approach room temperature. While this happens, we should pull out the seasonings. I like to include:
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Citrus Garlic Seasoning along with 5-6 garlic cloves
  • Montreal Steak Seasoning (From Costco. Any Canadian city will be sufficient.)
2. The Cooking
It is time. Place an oven safe pan on the stovetop and heat to the hightest possible heat along with 2tbsp of Olive Oil minimum (while the pan heats, preheat oven to 400). Once the heating is done, allow steak to sear on both sides for 2-3 mins. After this put the garlic cloves on the pan along with butter on one side of the steak and put this in the oven for 3:30 seconds. After this, remove from the oven, flip the steak, and add butter to the new side. Cook for another 3:30 seconds and depending on your steak width you will get the ultimate medium rare.

You're Done!

You've outdone yourself, the steak is complete! Let it sit for about 3 minutes end enjoy with complementary foods like potatos and broccoli. Thank your viewer for seeing my recipes and photos and goodluck to you in your endeavors.
